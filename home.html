Protect Our Oceans

SDG 14: Life Below Water - Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources

Our Mission & Goals

Why SDG 14 Matters Today

Oceans cover 70% of our planet and provide food, livelihoods, and oxygen. Yet they face unprecedented threats from pollution, overfishing, and climate change.

For students & communities: Our daily choices directly impact ocean health.

3 Actions You Can Take Today

  • Say no to single-use plastics
  • Choose sustainable seafood
  • Join local beach cleanups

If 1 million students did this: 50,000 kg less plastic waste

3 Positive Outcomes

  • Healthier marine ecosystems
  • Protected marine species
  • Sustainable fishing for future

Widely adopted: Oceans could recover by 2050

Quick Facts:

  • 🌊 8 million tons of plastic enter oceans yearly
  • 🐠 3 billion people depend on marine biodiversity
  • ⚓ 90% of fish stocks are overexploited
